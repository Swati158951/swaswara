A well-being destination from CGH Earth Experience Hotels

SwaSwara is located on Gokarana’s Om Beach – the pristine beach named after the first sound of creation “Om”. Crafted in the colours of the earth, SwaSwara offers a serene setting for holistic healing. Its form celebrates simplicity and purity, in keeping with the ethos of recuperation, rejuvenation and reconnection.

Swa Well-being Programme

Experience Swa Well-being – our signature holiday programme that rejuvenates you as you unwind amidst nature’s wonders. While you are here, you can go on serene nature trails, relax with massage therapies and yoga sessions, and try your hand at art and pottery. Or you can just put your feet up and read a book, with a refreshing glass of wine and the cool sea breeze to keep you company. Indulge in wholesome, flavourful cuisine, with dishes prepared using organic ingredients. Don’t miss the fresh catch of the day while you are here. The programme is designed to pamper your soul so that when you check out of this paradise by the sea, you are ready to take on the world with renewed energy

Programme Details

A well-being holiday with a flexible regimen

Swa Well-being begins with an introduction to Yogic Kriyas, Asanas, Yoga Nidra, Pranayama, meditation techniques, relaxing Ayurveda Abhyanga massages, guided art and pottery sessions and interactive cooking. This programme includes consultations with Ayurveda doctors and lifestyle guidance by our Yoga instructors and Ayurveda doctors. Supplemented by a healthy cuisine with a choice between fresh organic vegetarian food and fresh sea fish.

Ayurveda

Doctor's Consultation
Prior to the start of any Ayurveda therapy, there will be a brief consultation with the Ayurveda doctor.
Therapeutic Massages
Every patient is gently guided and supported through each aspect of the treatment they will undergo.
NOTE: For female guests, there will be restrictions on availing of treatments when menstruating.
Subject to the doctor’s approval, we offer the option of interchanging any of the treatments offered on the Swa-Wellbeing programme with those featured on our a la carte menu, available at the resort.
Supplementary costs – if any – for such exchanges will be charged extra as applicable.

Yoga & Meditation

Consultation with Yoga instructor
On arrival, the guests get a detailed consultation with the Yoga instructor.
Yoga sessions – A daily schedule of Yoga Asanas, Pranayama, Yoga Nidra and various guided meditation sessions are offered. Guests are encouraged to participate in these daily sessions as per their convenience.
We provide cotton Yoga wear to use during your stay.
Private Yoga sessions with a duration of 1 hour each can be purchased at the resort at an additional cost.

Living Spaces

Our spacious Konkan villas that boast an open inner courtyard and partially open to the sky bathrooms. To make the most of the beautiful natural surroundings only the bedrooms can be enclosed and are air conditioned; the rest of the villa opens out to nature.

Cuisine

The Swa Wellbeing programme includes all meals. SwaSwara offers healthy, gourmet cuisine which includes fresh organic vegetables and fresh seafood. Start your mornings with fragrant herbal teas and fresh fruits, offered at the Cocum restaurant from 6.00 to 7.00 am.

Art Therapy

Explore your creativity at SwaSwara’s art studio through a wide range of activities, including tribal Warli painting, water colours, origami and clay work on a traditional potter’s wheel. This interaction with art during your holiday will unleash your imagination and bring you closer to self-discovery.

Interactive Cooking

Our chefs offer interactive cooking classes every day, sharing their philosophies of healthy cooking without the use of refined sugar, flour or oils. The guests are encouraged to cook with our chefs and also take-home recipes and learnings that they can implement in their hometowns and cities towards improving the food habits and lifestyles of their families and loved ones.

Pick up

The price includes pick up and drop from Goa airport.
The distance between Goa's Dabolim airport and Swaswara is 158 km – a drive of approximately 3.5 to 4 hours.

Bird Watching

Guided bird watching walks are offered every Wednesday morning. With a protected patch of forest and the rainwater lakes, SwaSwara plays host to a wide variety of migratory and resident birds, such as the hornbill or the white-bellied sea eagle.
